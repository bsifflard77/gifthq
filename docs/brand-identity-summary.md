## Quick Reference

### Brand Name
- **Correct:** GiftHQ (one word, capital G, H, Q)
- **Never:** Gift HQ, Gifthq, gifthq, GIFTHQ

### Colors
| Color | Primary | Navy | Red | Gold |
|-------|---------|------|-----|------|
| Hex | — | #1E3A5F | #D64045 | #F4A300 |

**Tint scales:** Each color has 900→100 tint/shade scales for flexibility.

### Color Tokens (for devs)
- **Background:** bg-primary, bg-surface, bg-error
- **Text:** text-primary, text-secondary, text-link
- **Interactive:** cta-primary, cta-hover, border-focus
- **Status:** success, warning, error, info

## Logo System (Pages 7-11)

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space = height of "G" in logo
- Applies on all sides

### Approved Variants
1. Full color
2. 1-color Navy
3. 1-color White
4. Grayscale

### Favicon/App Icons
| Size | Simplification |
|------|----------------|
| 64px | Full logo |
| 32px | Simplified |
| 16px | Icon only |

### Logo Don'ts (12 violations listed)
- No stretching/distorting
- No unapproved colors
- No effects/shadows
- No rotation
- No busy backgrounds
- etc.

## The Thoughtful Ribbon (Pages 20-21)

**Our signature design element** — a flowing ribbon that represents the joy of gift-giving.

### Technical Specs
- Stroke weight: defined
- Curve style: flowing, organic
- Colors: Primary brand colors
- Opacity: varies by application

### 9 Approved Applications
1. Dividers
2. Progress bars
3. Overlays
4. Headers
5. Backgrounds
6. Accents
7. Icons
8. Buttons
9. Cards

**Don't:** Use as "random squiggles" or in unapproved ways.

## Microcopy Library (Pages 25-29)

### Success States (15)
- Saved, bought, wrapped, given, etc.
- Consistent tone: celebratory but not over-the-top

### Error States (17)
- Network, validation, search, auth, etc.
- Tone: helpful, not blaming

### Empty States (9)
- Each with title + body copy
- Encourage action without pressure

### Permission Requests (5)
- Full title/body/button copy
- **Critical for trust** — don't skip these

### Sensitive Moments (8)
- Budget warnings
- Duplicate gift alerts
- Deletion confirmations
- Handle with care

### Push Notifications & Email
- Example templates included
- Keep short, actionable
